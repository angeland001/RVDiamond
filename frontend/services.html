What I fix

Six things, done right.

I'm not a generalist with a tool belt. Every job below is something I do weekly, with the parts and diagnostic gear on the truck to finish in one visit.

No. 01

HVAC Repair

Roof A/C, furnaces, thermostats and ducting. Diagnosed and repaired at your site.

  • A/C not cooling
  • Furnace won't ignite
  • Capacitor & fan motor
  • Thermostat swap-outs
No. 02

Plumbing Repair

Fresh, gray and black water systems. Leaks, pumps, valves, water heaters.

  • Leak chase & reseal
  • Water pump replace
  • Hot water tank service
  • Toilet & seal rebuild
No. 03

Electrical Repair

12V house, shore power and converter/inverter systems. Safe, code-aware work.

  • Shore power issues
  • Converter & inverter
  • Battery & solar
  • Outlets, lights, breakers
No. 04

Winterizing

Done right the first time. Blow-out plus antifreeze, full system protection.

  • Air blow-out
  • RV antifreeze fill
  • Heater & ice maker
  • Storage prep
No. 05

De-Winterizing

Spring start-up — flush, sanitize, pressure-test, and a full systems check.

  • Flush & sanitize
  • Pressure test
  • Appliance check
  • Seal & roof inspection
No. 06

Pre-Purchase Inspection

Independent eyes before you sign. Written report covering structure, systems and safety.

  • Roof, seals, slides
  • All major systems
  • Safety & propane
  • Photos + written report

Got one of these? Let's get it handled.
